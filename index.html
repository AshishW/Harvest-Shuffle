How to Play

Objective:

Score as many points as you can in 30 moves.

Controls:

Click a tile, then click an adjacent tile to swap it.

Scoring:

Create a line of 3 or more matching items to score. Bigger matches and cascades yield higher points.

Tips:

Look for moves that set up cascades or create multiple matches at once.
